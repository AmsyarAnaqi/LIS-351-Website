Introduction

I always love travelling. As of now, I have travelled to more than 20 states here in the United States. It may not sounds a lot but I have only been here for less than 2 years. I always love visiting national parks all over US. Some of the national parks I have visited are:

  • Zion National Park
  • Grand Canyon National Park
  • Yosemite National Park
  • Rocky Mountain National Park
  • Bryce Canyon National Park
  • Niagara Falls State Park

I am an annual pass holder for America The Beautiful, which is a pass that grants you entrance to all lands managed by the Bureau of Land Management, Bureau of Reclamation and U.S. Army Corps of Engineers. If you are an avid national parks explorer, you should get this pass since it will save you a lot of money. If you are interested in buying or learning about this pass, you should visit the National Park Service website.
